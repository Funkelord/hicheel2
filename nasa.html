NASA’s SpaceX Crew-8

After safely splashing down off the Florida coast on Friday, Oct. 25, a NASA astronaut experienced a medical issue. Following an overnight stay at Ascension Sacred Heart Pensacola in Florida, the astronaut was released and returned to NASA’s Johnson Space Center in Houston Saturday, Oct. 26. The crew member is in good health and will resume normal post-flight reconditioning with other crew members.

Earth Information Center

For more than 50 years, NASA satellites have provided data on Earth's land, water, air, temperature, and climate. NASA's Earth Information Center allows visitors to see how our planet is changing in six key areas: sea level rise and coastal impacts, health and air quality, wildfires, greenhouse gases, sustainable energy, and agriculture.
